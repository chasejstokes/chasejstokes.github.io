Instructions

Adding Text: Type your desired text in the box below. Select its role from the choices below the box. Click the "Add Text" button to add the text to the chart. The sizes of text are pre-determined and not possible to change.

Annotation Caption Title

Moving Text: Once the text is added to the chart, click and drag to position where you want. "Annotations" will automatically add to the center of the chart.

Deleting Text: Double-click anywhere in the text to remove it.

Saving Your Design: When you are finished with your design, click the button below to automatically download the image to your computer. Otherwise, you can right click and select "Save Image As". You will upload this file in the next question and then can remove it from your device.
